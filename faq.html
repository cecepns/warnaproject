How do we book your Bali photographer packages?

We've made our booking process simple and convenient. To reserve your date, a 50% non-refundable deposit is required. For weddings, the remaining balance is due after the wedding day. For family or engagement sessions, you can pay the remaining amount after the photoshoot.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept payment via WISE (most recommended) and bank transfer. We'll send all the details along with the invoice when you have confirmed the booking.
